FAQ

Axmol currently supports recurring donations via OSC (Open Source Collective), PayPal and GitHub Sponsors.

To cancel a PayPal subscription, please log in to your PayPal account at paypal.com, then go to: Settings > Automatic Payments > Manage Automatic Payments, and cancel the one labeled Axmol Foundation.

To cancel a recurring GitHub sponsorship, visit github.com/sponsors, click on Your Sponsorships, locate Axmol Engine, and choose Cancel Sponsorship from the options menu.

To cancel a recurring Open Source Collective (OSC) contribution, log in to your account at opencollective.com, go to Profile > Subscriptions, find your monthly Axmol contribution, and click Cancel.

If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact us at support@simdsoft.com with your PayPal, GitHub, or OSC account email and your current membership level.

At present, Axmol Engine does not operate its own nonprofit entity. Instead, we rely on the Open Source Collective (OSC), a U.S.-registered 501(c)(6) nonprofit fiscal host dedicated to supporting open source projects.

By sponsoring Axmol through OSC, Corporate contributors benefit from transparent financial reporting, proper invoicing, and compliance with international nonprofit standards. While contributions made via OSC are generally not tax-deductible, the structure ensures accountability and provides formal invoices, which are valuable for internal reimbursement and auditing purposes.

This arrangement ensures accountability and makes it easier for companies to justify sponsorship expenses during audits. In contrast, PayPal and GitHub Sponsors are designed primarily for individual backers and are not suitable for large Corporate payments.
