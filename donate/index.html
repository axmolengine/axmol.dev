Axmol Engine

A Multi-platform Engine for Mobile, Desktop and Xbox.

Donate to Axmol

Axmol Engine is an open-source, C++ multi-platform engine designed for mobile devices, desktop, and Xbox, well-suited for 2D game development. It was launched in November 2019 as a fork of Cocos2d-x v4.0.

Why need your donation?

  • We are axmol engine fans and developers from the world.
  • We are working for axmol and make it for free and mostly for having fun.
  • Each detected bug/issue can also be on a roadmap and not fixed as fast as possible, we try to fix as fast as possible, so a small donation is a possible way to push some wishes on the top.
